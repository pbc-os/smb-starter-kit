# Creative Matrix — Starter Set Example

Example output for a **premium butcher shop** using the 6-concept starter set. This shows the expected output format and level of detail for each concept. Names and neighborhoods in this example are fictional.

**Brand:** Highland Butcher Co (urban neighborhood butcher shop, premium meats, local sourcing)
**Offer:** Free delivery on first order over $50
**Audience:** Health-conscious urban foodies, 28-45, who care about where their food comes from

---

## Concept 1 — TOF x Static x Pain Point

**Hook/Headline:** "You don't know where your grocery store meat comes from. We do."
**Body Copy:** Every cut at Highland Butcher is sourced from farms we visit personally. No mystery meat. No middlemen. Just honest beef, pork, and poultry from people we trust.
**CTA:** Shop now — free delivery on your first order over $50.
**Sub-Angle:** Frustration
**Triggers:** Loss aversion (what you're missing), embarrassment (not knowing)
**Format Spec:** 1080x1350 static image. Hero shot of a beautifully marbled ribeye on butcher paper with the farm name written in chalk underneath.
**Targeting:** Broad — food/cooking interests, local metro geo, ages 25-50
**Priority:** High

---

## Concept 2 — TOF x Video x Desire

**Hook/Headline:** "This is what dinner looks like when you buy from a real butcher."
**Body Copy:** Searing a dry-aged NY strip from a farm 90 miles away. No plastic wrap. No fluorescent lights. Just meat the way it's supposed to be.
**CTA:** Free delivery on your first order. Link in bio.
**Sub-Angle:** Aspiration
**Triggers:** Ambition (better cooking), status (elevated food experience)
**Format Spec:** 1080x1920 vertical video, 15 seconds. Open with close-up sizzle shot (first 3 seconds critical). Quick cuts: butcher wrapping the steak → home kitchen → plated dinner.
**Targeting:** Broad — cooking enthusiasts, food content viewers, local metro
**Priority:** High

---

## Concept 3 — TOF x Carousel x Social Proof

**Hook/Headline:** "Why 2,000+ local families stopped buying meat at the grocery store."
**Body Copy:**
- Card 1: "We asked our customers why they switched. Here's what they said."
- Card 2: "I can actually taste the difference." — Maria, Oakhill
- Card 3: "My kids eat more vegetables now because the protein is so good it makes the whole plate better." — James, Riverbend
- Card 4: "I spend less because I waste less. Better meat = actually eating it all." — Priya, Elmwood
- Card 5: Free delivery on your first order over $50. Shop now.
**CTA:** Try it yourself — free delivery on first order.
**Sub-Angle:** Testimony
**Triggers:** Trust, social proof, FOMO (everyone else figured it out)
**Format Spec:** 1080x1080 per card, 5 cards. Clean typography on brand color backgrounds. Customer photos if available, otherwise styled quote cards.
**Targeting:** Broad — parents, foodies, local metro neighborhoods
**Priority:** High

---

## Concept 4 — MOF x Video x Social Proof

**Hook/Headline:** "Watch how we break down a whole animal in 4 minutes."
**Body Copy:** Most grocery stores get pre-cut boxes. We get whole animals from local farms and break them down in-house. That's why you get cuts you can't find anywhere else — and why nothing goes to waste.
**CTA:** See what's available this week. Free delivery on first order.
**Sub-Angle:** Authority
**Triggers:** Credibility (showing the craft), FOMO (exclusive cuts)
**Format Spec:** 1080x1350 video, 30 seconds. Real footage of butcher working. Close-up knife work, steam, the case being filled. Minimal text overlay — let the craft speak.
**Targeting:** Retargeting — people who visited site or engaged with TOF ads
**Priority:** Medium

---

## Concept 5 — MOF x Static x Desire

**Hook/Headline:** "Your Saturday dinner, elevated."
**Body Copy:** Dry-aged bone-in ribeye. Heritage pork chops. House-made sausages. Everything you need for the weekend — sourced locally, cut fresh, delivered to your door.
**CTA:** Order by Thursday for Saturday delivery.
**Sub-Angle:** Freedom
**Triggers:** Independence (no more grocery store trips), relief (weekend sorted)
**Format Spec:** 1080x1350 static. Flat-lay of a weekend spread: 3-4 premium cuts arranged on butcher paper with herbs, salt, a cast iron pan. Warm lighting, slightly overhead angle.
**Targeting:** Retargeting — site visitors, email subscribers, past engagers
**Priority:** Medium

---

## Concept 6 — BOF x Static x Pain Point (with offer)

**Hook/Headline:** "Still thinking about it? Your first order ships free."
**Body Copy:** Free delivery on orders over $50. Try our best-seller box: 2 ribeyes, 1 lb ground beef, 4 chicken thighs. Everything sourced from farms within 150 miles.
**CTA:** Use code FIRST50 at checkout. Offer expires Sunday.
**Sub-Angle:** Regret
**Triggers:** Loss aversion (offer expiring), sunk cost (you've already been thinking about it)
**Format Spec:** 1080x1080 static. Product shot of the best-seller box with price and free delivery badge. Clear, bold offer text. Urgency element (expiration).
**Targeting:** Retargeting — cart abandoners, high-intent site visitors, email clickers
**Priority:** High

## Recommended Test Order

1. Launch concepts 1, 2, 3 simultaneously (TOF — cast a wide net)
2. After 3-5 days with data, launch concepts 4, 5 (MOF — retarget engagers)
3. After 7 days, launch concept 6 (BOF — convert warm audience)

## Benchmarks to Watch

- **Video hook rate:** Kill below 25%, good at 30-40%, scale above 40%
- **Static conversion share:** Should drive 60-70% of total conversions
- **Carousel swipe-through:** Good carousels get 1.4x higher engagement than single image
- **Overall:** If CPA on any concept is 2x+ your target after $200 spend, pause and iterate
